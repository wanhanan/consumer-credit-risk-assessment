# Retail Bank Consumer Credit Risk Assessment

## Project Overview

This project simulates a real-world consulting engagement for a retail bank seeking to improve its consumer lending decisions through data analytics.

The objective is to understand consumer credit risk, identify factors associated with loan default, develop a predictive credit risk model, and communicate findings through interactive dashboards and business recommendations.

---

## Business Problem

Retail banks face financial losses when borrowers fail to repay their loans. The challenge is to balance profitable lending while minimizing credit losses.

---

## Project Objectives

- Understand consumer credit risk
- Analyze historical loan data
- Identify key drivers of loan default
- Develop a predictive credit risk model
- Build an interactive dashboard
- Provide business recommendations
