# Business Understanding

## Client

ABC Retail Bank

## Business Problem

ABC Retail Bank has experienced an increase in consumer loan defaults. As more borrowers fail to repay their loans, the bank experiences higher financial losses and reduced profitability.Management wants to better identify high-risk applicants before approving loans.

##  Business Objectives

- Develop a data-driven approach that estimates the probability that a borrower will default.
- The model should support lending decisions while balancing profitability and credit risk.

## Key Business Questions

- What factors increase the likelihood of default?
- Which borrowers are considered high risk?
- Which variables are the strongest predictors?
- Can historical loan data predict future defaults?
- How can the bank improve lending decisions?

## Success Criteria
The project is successful if it helps the bank:

- Generate actionable borrower insights that improve credit decisioning, risk management, and customer engagement strategies
- Identify high-risk applicants
- Reduce loan defaults
- Support consistent lending decisions
